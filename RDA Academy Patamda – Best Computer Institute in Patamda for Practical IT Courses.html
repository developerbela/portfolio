OUR POPULAR COURSES

Explore Our Most In-Demand Courses! View All

DCA

The DCA (Diploma in Computer Applications) course teaches essential computer skills like MS Office, Internet use, Tally basics, and data entry. It is a beginner-friendly, job-oriented program designed to help students build strong computer knowledge for office and daily digital tasks.

ADCA

The ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) course provides advanced training in computers, including MS Office, Tally with GST, advanced Excel, graphic design, internet tools, and basic programming. It is a job-oriented course designed to build strong technical skills for office work, accounting, designing, and digital careers.

C/C++

The C/C++ Programming course teaches the fundamentals of coding, including variables, loops, functions, pointers, OOP concepts, and data structures. It is ideal for beginners who want to build a strong foundation in programming and prepare for careers in software development, game development, and technical fields.

DCTT

The Diploma in Computer Teacher’s Training (DCTT) course prepares students to become skilled computer instructors by teaching fundamentals of computers, MS Office, internet tools, teaching methods, classroom management, and basic programming. It is ideal for those who want to build a career as a computer teacher or trainer in schools and institutes.

DFA

The Diploma in Financial Accounting course provides practical training in Tally with GST, accounting basics, bookkeeping, payroll, invoicing, and financial reporting. It is designed for students and job seekers who want to build strong accounting skills for office, business, and finance-related jobs.

DIT

The Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) course offers essential training in computer fundamentals, networking, software applications, web basics, and IT tools. It is ideal for students who want to build strong technical skills and start a career in the IT and computer industry.

CCA

The Certificate in Computer Application (CCA) course provides basic training in computers, including MS Office, internet use, email handling, and essential digital skills. It is perfect for beginners who want to learn practical computer knowledge for office work and daily digital tasks.

BCC

The Basic Computer Course teaches essential computer skills like MS Office, typing, internet browsing, email handling, and basic digital operations. It is ideal for beginners who want to confidently use computers for daily tasks, studies, and office work.
